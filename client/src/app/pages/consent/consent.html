Biometric Data Consent

Please read carefully before proceeding

📷
What we collect

Your webcam will capture your face and convert it into a set of 128 numbers (a mathematical descriptor). We do not store any photos of your face.

🎯
How it's used

These numbers are compared against event photos to find pictures you appear in. This data is used solely for photo matching within this app.

🗑️
Auto-deletion

Your biometric data is automatically and permanently deleted after 7 days. You can also delete it at any time from your account.

🔐
Security

Data is stored securely in our database and never shared with third parties. Only you can trigger a face match.

By clicking I Agree, you consent to the collection and processing of your biometric data as described above, in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
